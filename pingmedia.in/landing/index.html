It removes the guesswork and identifies opportunities you may not be aware of. It discovers the path of least resistance (the fastest, easiest, most practical way for the business to achieve its goal), and it sets out clear objectives and KPI’s so that we all know we’re on the right track.

do the groundwork

The Business Growth Strategy is an in-depth process which takes 6 to 8 weeks to complete. It covers the full Grow Trifecta (Marketing, Sales & Retention). To begin, we need to do a lot of digging:

business metrics

WHERE DOES THE MONEY TRULY COME FROM?
We delve deep into your business to understand your revenue, lifetime value, lifespan and profit margin across specific services, company sizes, verticals and channels.

We reveal who your greatest customers truly are and then define a path to win more of them.

historic performance

LET’S NOT MAKE THE SAME MISTAKES TWICE.
We find it useful to understand how your previous initiatives turned out. What worked and what failed? These successes and failures will be built into the future strategy so that the past gives value to your future.

sales performance

HOW EFFECTIVE IS YOUR SALES STRATEGY?
What is the structure of the sales pipeline? Where are deals dropping? How do you sell? What is your pricing and packaging model?

We’ll explore your full sales process to ensure all leads generated have the greatest chance to be converted into sales.

brand audit

WILL YOUR BRAND SUPPORT OR HINDER?
A strong brand builds trust and improves all marketing & sales metrics. A weak brand erodes them.

That’s why your brand strength has such a profound affect on your growth prospects and we need to make sure it’s strong.

customer surveys

WE’LL SPEAK DIRECTLY TO YOUR CUSTOMERS
How do they see you, where do they get their value, and why did they chose to work with you in the first place? How did they find out about you, and what were their main pain points?

These questions will give us key insights on how to win more customers like these.

website audit

ALL MARKETING INITIATIVES LEAD HERE
Your website is a vital piece of the growth puzzle. Increasing your site’s conversion rate from 1% to 2% will double your leads.

We’ll assess your website across SEO, UX, Psychology, Compatibility & Speed to identify areas for improvement and maximise the potential growth of your business.

business objectives

WHERE DO YOU WANT TO BE IN 12 MONTHS?
What are the revenue targets? How many new clients do you want to have? How much do you want average spend to increase?

Once we know the destination we can work backwards and define a path to get there.

customer objectives

FINDING THE SWEET SPOT
The crossover between what you want and what your ideal customers want. If both objectives match, we’ll be able to form the ideal strategy that resonates with your perfect customers and achieves your greatest objectives.

We can speak their language and tick their boxes, making the whole process so much smoother.

challenges

PREDICT THE OBSTACLES BEFORE THEY ARRIVE
What might get in the way on our journey towards growth? What’s likely to hold us back? What could go wrong? How can we overcome these challenges and maximise your chances of success?

We’ll develop a set of contingencies to sidestep common challenges if they arise.

business growth strategy
